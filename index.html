We sink seaweed to stabilize the climate

Phykos is an ocean carbon dioxide removal company.
We amplify natural marine carbon cycles to remove excess atmospheric CO2 at climate relevant scale.

How It Works

We cultivate seaweed and sink it deep in the ocean, mimicking a carbon sequestration process that occurs naturally with coastal seaweed beds and safely sequestering excess carbon for 1,000 years or more; reducing the danger of catastrophic climate change.

Grow

Our robotic seaweed growth platforms maintain position or migrate with changing conditions to optimize growth. Seaweed photosynthesis converts dissolved CO2 into organic carbon embodied in the seaweed.

Algorithms maximize growth on our platforms while avoiding sensitive ocean environments and other ocean users. Our system works in harmony with seasonal shifts in the ocean offering a safe and scalable pathway for nature based carbon dioxide removal.

Sink

The seaweed from our growth platforms is harvested and sinks into the deep ocean, carrying the absorbed carbon with it to the deep ocean.

We target our seaweed harvests to regions deeper than 1500 meters of depth to ensure 1,000 years of durable carbon sequestration. High resolution ocean models guide our platforms to deposition regions which maximize carbon storage while minimizing disturbance to the deep ocean environment. Platform sensors and satellite communications enable precise reporting of our progress.

Save

The carbon embodied in our sunken seaweed is effectively removed from the atmosphere as the surface ocean and atmosphere return to equilibrium. We sustain this process by selling carbon offsets to help organizations, companies, and governments meet their climate protection commitments, but the real payoff is a healthy planet and a sustainable future for humanity.

As a bonus, our nature based carbon removal process has the added benefits of cleaning, cooling, and de-acidifying ocean waters while providing protective habitat for marine life!
